# Create a new Knowledge Base article

Source: https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/create-new-knowledge-base-article

To create a new article, right-click and open this link (https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/new) in a new tab. The Create a New Knowledge Base Article page will open. Here's what the top part of that page looks like:

Don't feel overwhelmed by all of the fields and checkboxes when creating a new article and just focus on your content. To get started, here is what you have to do:

- Fill out key metadata fields including title, slug, category, product(s), topic, and search result summary. See Article Metadata and how to edit (/en-US/kb/article-metadata) for more guidance on how to complete these fields.
- Go to the Content box and start writing!
- Click Preview Content to see what your article will look like when it’s been published.
- Be sure to double check on the other details (title, slug, category, product, topic, etc) before clicking on the Submit button. When you’re satisfied with your article's content and appearance, click Submit for Review.
- Briefly describe your changes in the Submit your Changes box that appears. If you have more work to do, you can type in “Work in progress”.
- Click Submit.
