# Thunderbird house conventions

These Thunderbird-specific conventions OVERRIDE the general SUMO style guidance wherever they conflict. Apply them when generating drafts.

## Menus — always use the app menu, never the menu bar

- Thunderbird's classic menu bar (File, Edit, View, …) is hidden by default and is **not cross-platform** — Windows and Linux may not show it at all. **Never instruct users to use the menu bar.**
- Always reach menu commands through the Thunderbird **app menu** (the hamburger button). Begin such a step like:
  `Click Thunderbird app menu {menu ☰}`
  and then continue the path, e.g.:
  `Click Thunderbird app menu {menu ☰} > {menu View} > {menu Layout}`
